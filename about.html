About NoCo Tech Coalition

Mission

As the Northern Colorado Tech community continues to grow, we've found a need for a unified calendar. Most people in the tech industry wear many hats, so a single group may cover all the interests of an individual. "We" (including you, the reader) serve to provide a single point of reference for all of the tech groups in Colorado north of the Denver metro (we'll call this latitudes north of boulder county for now).

Community Groups

The NoCo Tech Coalition hosts events from various tech communities throughout Northern Colorado, including:

  • NoCo Hackers - A community of cybersecurity professionals, enthusiasts, and playful computing practitioners. Meets monthly to share knowledge and collaborate on security topics.
